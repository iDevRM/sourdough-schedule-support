Frequently asked questions

How does backward planning work?

Set your target bake finish time and Sourdough Schedule works backward through every stage — starter feeding, levain, bulk fermentation, shaping, and proofing — so each step lands right on time.

Does temperature affect my schedule?

Yes. Fermentation speed changes with dough temperature, so setting your sitting temperature adjusts the timing of each stage for a more accurate plan.

How do I use the Home Screen widget?

Long-press your Home Screen, tap the + button, search for “Sourdough Schedule,” and add the widget. It shows your next step and when it's due at a glance.

Will I get reminders for each step?

Yes. Enable notifications when prompted (or in iOS Settings → Sourdough Schedule → Notifications) to get a reminder as each stage comes due.

Do I need an account?

You can start planning a bake right away. An account is only needed for features that sync or unlock premium options.

Which devices are supported?

Sourdough Schedule runs on iPhone and iPad with iOS 17 or later.
