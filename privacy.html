Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sourdough Schedule app (“the app,” “we,” “us”) handles information when you use it. We aim to collect as little as possible and to be clear about what we do collect.

Information you create in the app

Your bake schedules, target times, temperature settings, and related preferences are stored on your device. This content is used to provide the app's core scheduling features. If account sync is enabled, this data may also be stored in our backend to make it available across your devices.

Account information

If you choose to create an account or sign in, we process the information needed to authenticate you (such as an email address or a sign-in identifier from your chosen provider). You can use the app's core planning features without an account.

Analytics and diagnostics

We use Google Firebase to understand how the app is used and to diagnose crashes and errors so we can improve reliability. This may include aggregated usage events, app performance data, crash logs, and device/OS information (for example, device model and iOS version). This information helps us fix bugs and prioritize features. Google's handling of this data is governed by the Firebase Privacy and Security and Google Privacy Policy.

How we use information

  • To provide and maintain the app's scheduling features
  • To sync your data across devices when you sign in
  • To diagnose crashes and improve performance and stability
  • To understand feature usage and guide future improvements

What we do not do

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your bake data for advertising.

Data sharing

We share information only with service providers that help us operate the app (such as Google Firebase for backend, authentication, analytics, and crash reporting), and where required by law. These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms.

Data retention

On-device data remains until you delete it or remove the app. If you have an account, you may request deletion of your account data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your choices

You can disable notifications in iOS Settings, and you can request access to or deletion of account data by emailing us. You may also manage app tracking and data settings through iOS privacy controls.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
